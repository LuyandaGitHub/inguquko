ABOUT US

Inguquko means Change. Inguquko Consulting Agency is a South African based privately owned consulting agency that was established in 2016 and was registered in 2018. The aim was to provide good quality servives needed by our clients in the Accounting, Payroll, Branding, and IT industry. The company inherently has 100% black management and owns a BEE status comprising of two black male directors and co-founders.



Our company offers a list of services for small and medium businesses, those who want to open new ventures and for already established businesses depending on their particular needs which may include: Accounting services, Tax services, Payroll services and IT services. the company's overall technique is based on a continuing improvement process of setting goals, calculating results and providing reviews to accomplish further growth and progress. Over the past years, we have worked with sseveral companies and the feedback we recieve is always overwhelming as clients always compliment the exceptional service we always offer.
